Eligibility

Do You Qualify?

Most applicants who meet the basic criteria below are approved at some level. We work to find a solution even for complex situations.

  • 18 years or older (or legal adult in your jurisdiction)
  • Valid government-issued photo ID
  • Proof of income (employment, business, or other verifiable source)
  • Active bank account for disbursement
  • No active bankruptcy proceedings
  • Completed eligibility screening and KYC process
